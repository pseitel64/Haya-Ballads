The Place You Come From — Justinian Mugasha

The Place You Come From is rich in poetic imagery. Indeed, the proper use and interpretation of metaphor is the pivot on which the action turns. As in the ballads King Kaitaba and Mugasha, the dramatic energy that drives the plot forward begins with a controlled verbal insult. Narrative action includes sex, accusation, judicial judgment, and explications of poetic imagery.
